How does Codecov combine matrix builds and multiple CI providers?

Flawlessly. Codecov merges builds in to a single report while maintaining the original source of the coverage data. Send as many uploads from differnt CI providers and languages to Codecov, plug-and-play! See a fine example here pyca/cryptography.

Does Codecov replace my CI provider?

No, Codecov does not "test" your code, thats the job of the CI provider. Codecov does static anaylsis on your repository once your tests are complete and coverage reports are uploaded.
